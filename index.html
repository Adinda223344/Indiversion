What is Indiversion?

INDIVERSION 2025 adalah ajang budaya internasional yang merayakan kekayaan warisan lebih dari 300 kelompok etnis dari Indonesia. Mengusung tema "Chronicles of the Past, Vision of the Future", acara ini menampilkan tari tradisional, musik daerah, busana etnik, hingga pertunjukan teater.

Lebih dari sekadar hiburan, INDIVERSION 2025 menjadi wadah untuk memperkenalkan dan merayakan khazanah budaya Indonesia, menumbuhkan rasa bangga, serta mendorong generasi muda untuk melestarikan dan mewariskannya ke masa depan.

Tari Baksa Kembang
Tari Baksa Kembang is a traditional Banjar dance from South Kalimantan, Indonesia. It's a welcoming dance often performed by female dancers, typically in odd numbers, to greet guests, especially those of high standing. The dance depicts the beauty of flowers and the grace of young women in a garden.

Pakarena
Pakarena is a traditional dance from South Sulawesi, Indonesia, performed to express gratitude and respect to the spirits. The dancers move gracefully with slow and repetitive steps, holding fans as a symbol of elegance.

Cakalele
Cakalele is a traditional Indonesian war dance originating from North and Central Maluku. It is performed by male dancers, often with two representing opposing leaders or captains, accompanied by warriors. The dance is known for its dynamic movements, including jumping, turning, stomping, and sword-slashing, reflecting its historical significance in warfare. While traditionally a war dance, in some regions, like Banda, it also incorporates Islamic religious and ethical values.
